# Product Overview

The Arithmetic Practice Portal is a privacy-first mathematics practice platform for Danish public school students (grades 0-9). The platform aligns with the official Danish "Fælles Mål" mathematics curriculum and provides infinite exercise variations through a template-based generation system.

## Key Features

- **Anonymous Authentication**: UUID-based system with no personal data collection
- **Bilingual Support**: Danish (primary) and English from launch
- **Curriculum-Aligned**: Mapped to Danish Fælles Mål competency areas and skills
- **Offline-Capable**: Full PWA with service worker and IndexedDB
- **Adaptive Learning**: Spaced repetition system (SRS) with mastery tracking
- **Progressive Hints**: Four-level hint system without penalties
- **Privacy-First**: No tracking, GDPR compliant by design

## Target Users

Students in Danish public schools, grades 0-9 (klassetrin), practicing mathematics independently at home or school.
