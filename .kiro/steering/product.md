# Product Overview

## Arithmetic Practice Portal

A privacy-first, accessible mathematics practice platform for Danish students (grades 4-9) with comprehensive offline support.

## Core Features

- **Anonymous Authentication**: UUID-based system with no personal data collection
- **Adaptive Learning**: Spaced repetition system (SRS) with mastery tracking across 5 levels
- **Offline-First**: Full PWA with IndexedDB storage and background sync
- **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 AA compliant with screen reader support, dyslexia-friendly fonts, and 44px touch targets
- **Bilingual**: Danish (primary) and English support with culturally appropriate exercise contexts
- **Exercise System**: Template-based generation with hints, worked solutions, and visual aids

## Target Users

- Primary: Danish students in grades 4-9
- Secondary: Teachers and parents monitoring progress
- Accessibility: Students with visual impairments, dyslexia, or motor difficulties

## Key Requirements

- Privacy-first design (no email, name, or age collection)
- Works offline with automatic sync when online
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Comprehensive hint system (4 levels including worked solutions)
- Progress tracking with mastery levels and streak calculations
- Supabase backend for data persistence and cross-device sync
